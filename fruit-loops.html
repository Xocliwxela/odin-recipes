Fruit Loops

Description

A delicious blend of crunchy fruit flavored loops and cold cow milk. mmmmmm!

Ingredients

  • Fruit Loops Cereal
  • Cold Milk, preferably from a cow or an Almond
  • A bowl to put it in

Steps

  1. Grab bowl
  2. Pour Fruit Loops into bowl (DONT OVERFILL!!)
  3. Cover cereal and fill bowl with your choice of cold milk
  4. Dig in!!
