Buttered Toast

Description

A warm, buttery, toasty, bready treat

Ingredients

  • Fresh Bread
  • Butter
  • A Toaster

Steps

  1. Put bread in toaster, toast until your heart is content (DONT BURN)
  2. Coat freshly toasted bread with butter, covering evenly
  3. Wait for toast to cool and butter to melt
  4. Dig in!!
